about me

Hi, I'm Dan — AKA Wig.

Amongst other things, I'm a digital learning content designer and learning experience curator — and the founder of Forged Frameworks. Past the job titles: I take complex, regulated subject matter and forge it into learning people actually enjoy.

case study · l3 asbestos itm

A compliance-ready build, measured end to end

Inside the Level 3 Asbestos Industry Technical Module (ITMIndustry Technical Module (ITM) — developed to support the specific Level 3 Laboratory Technician Apprenticeship Standard (ST0248).S17 (Skill) — identify relevant information from scientific sources (e.g. supervisors, literature, digital tools) in order to contribute to problem-solving.K21 (Knowledge) — demonstrate theoretical knowledge of a named or recognised scientific subject appropriate to the workplace sector (e.g. pharmacology, biotechnology, dental, or chemical)., Rise 360) — enabling Bulk & Analysis and Air Analysts toward the L3 lab-technician standard and RSPH qualifications.

95%+

Completion rate across the first cohorts

Pre/post

Objective knowledge-gain measures, not just averages

L3

Aligned to lab-technician standard & RSPH qualifications

1Challenge

  • Asbestos is a high-risk material in construction and maintenance — L3 learners needed a module that was engaging, compliance-ready and measurable.
  • Previous training was inconsistent, with few interactive elements and no objective assessment data.

2Actions

  • Designed and authored a fully online ITM using structured frameworks.
  • Embedded AI-supported resources — transcripts, HTML coding, podcast-style retention tools.
  • Scenario-based formative assessments and knowledge checks aligned to apprenticeship standards.
  • Built accessibility-first — alt text, subtitles, screen-reader compatibility.

3Outcomes

  • High completion within the first cohorts — over 95%.
  • Clear knowledge gain through objective pre- and post-assessment measures.
  • Positive learner feedback; employers recognised improved compliance assurance.
  • Established as a model for future health & safety ITMs.

gen-ai pipeline

The Catalyst Pipeline

An agentic, human-assured pipeline that turns a single curriculum platform document — a Digital Content Plan, Module Narrative or Scheme of Work — into a complete, level-calibrated set of ready-to-review learning documents — faster, more consistently, and without ever losing the subject expert's voice. The inner workings are the secret sauce; here's what it delivers.

1

Ingest

Your curriculum platform documents go in as-is — Digital Content Plans, Module Narratives or Schemes of Work — read and structured with no reformatting on your side.

2

Generate

Builds a full, aligned document set for every session from that single source.

3

Calibrate

Tunes tone, depth and professional context to the learner's level (L3–L6) and sector.

4

Assure

Layered, human-aligned QA catches and auto-repairs issues before anything reaches you.

5

Publish

Polished, branded Word outputs plus learner resources — consistent every time.
