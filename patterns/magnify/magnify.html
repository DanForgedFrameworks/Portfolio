Turn on inspect mode, then move over the image to explore details. Use your mouse wheel or + / − to zoom on desktop. On touch devices, drag your finger across the image. If the magnifier ever looks faint or disappears after moving off the image, move back over the picture or toggle “Enable inspect mode” off and on to reset it.
